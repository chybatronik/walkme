WalkMe Editor

Using WalkMe’s advanced Editor, you can create Walk-Thrus within minutes, without any technical knowledge, all by using a simple point-and-click interface. Click here to download WalkMe Editor for Firefox browser

Browser Support

WalkMe supports all major browsers.

Walk-Thru Features

  • Welcome Screen Customize your welcome screen to greet your user to your Walk-Thru.

  • Redirect Redirect your user’s browser to any URL in your website

  • Auto Start Automatically play your Walk-Thru upon site entrance

  • Skip First Step Guide users directly to your second step, instantly

Full API

WalkMe delivers a full API, which allows you to utilize your imagination. Play your Walk-Thrus using your own buttons or links. You may find relevant information at our Wiki

Step Features

Balloon Position Set your instruction balloons for your Walk-Thru in different locations on the website.
Skippable Allow your users to skip steps without harming the Walk-Thru process.

Step Triggers

In order to support any possible business process, the WalkMe Editor delivers a set of step triggers, which allows you to specify when a step is finished and when it is time to advance to the next step. In each step, you should set the step trigger according to the business process itself.
